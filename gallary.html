Afghan Proverbs Gallery

A visual collection of traditional Afghan proverbs in Pashto and Persian, capturing the rich wisdom and guidance of everyday life.

«.بعد از هر تاریکی روشنی است»🔸

Literal Translation:
"After every darkness, there is light."

Message:
No matter how dark the moment, brighter days will follow.

«.آزموده را آزمودن خطاست» 🔸

Literal Translation:
"To test the tested is a mistake."

Message:
Trust the proven — avoid wasting time on repeated doubt.

".د صبر مېوه خواږه وي، خو ریښه‌ یې تریوه ده"🔸

Literal Translation:
"The fruit of patience is sweet, but its root is bitter."

Message:
Enduring hardship leads to sweet and meaningful results.

".بې عمله علم، د خره پر شا کتاب دی" 🔸

Literal Translation:
"Knowledge without action is like a book on a donkey’s back."

Message:
True wisdom lies not just in knowing, but in doing.
